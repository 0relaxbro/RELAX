How this actually works

  • We fetch a wallet's on-chain activity from the last 30 days directly from Solana's public RPC — the same free, open network any wallet app uses. Very active wallets are capped at the most recent 60 transactions to keep this fast and free.
  • We look at swap frequency, how many different tokens were touched, and how tightly clustered the trades are in time.
  • That gets turned into a simple, readable score — for fun and self-reflection, not financial advice.
  • No connection to the RELAX token contract, its authority, or its supply. This is a separate analysis layer, not a token feature.
  • The public RPC is shared by the whole world and rate-limited. If it's busy, the tool will say so instead of pretending to work.
